How to Keep Your Jewelry Sparkling

March 15, 2024 By Sanskriti Team

Preserve the brilliance of your precious pieces with our expert care guides. From daily wear advice to deep cleaning routines, learn how to keep your jewels looking as radiant as ever.

Daily Care Tips

  • Store your jewelry in a cool, dry place
  • Keep pieces separate to prevent scratching
  • Remove jewelry before swimming or showering
  • Clean regularly with appropriate solutions

Deep Cleaning Guide

Regular maintenance is key to preserving your jewelry's beauty. Here's a step-by-step guide to deep cleaning your precious pieces:

  1. Use a soft-bristled brush for delicate pieces
  2. Choose appropriate cleaning solutions for different metals
  3. Pay special attention to settings and crevices
  4. Dry thoroughly with a soft, lint-free cloth

Professional Care

While regular maintenance is important, professional cleaning and inspection should be done annually. This ensures your jewelry remains in perfect condition and helps identify any potential issues early.
